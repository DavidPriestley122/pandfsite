Priestley & Ferraro

Early Chinese Art  ·  St James's, London  ·  1994–2026

Priestley & Ferraro opened in the autumn of 1994, born from an unexpected partnership between two former Sotheby's colleagues, and for thirty-one years we occupied our corner of London's Chinese art world — first on Davies Street, then King Street, and finally at 3 Bury Street, St James's. We specialised in early Chinese ceramics, particularly those of the Song dynasty, and over three decades we were privileged to work with collectors, scholars and fellow dealers from across the world who shared our fascination with the unusual, the mysterious, and the beautiful.

Benedicta Ferraro's passing in July 2023 marked our true end. Without Benny, there could be no Priestley & Ferraro. We are grateful beyond measure to all those who placed their trust in our judgement, and who became, so many of them, friends.

David Priestley, March 2026

普利斯特利與費拉羅畫廊於一九九四年秋季創立，由兩位前蘇富比同事攜手合作，在倫敦中國藝術界共同耕耘三十一年——先在戴維斯街，後遷至國王街，最終落戶聖詹姆斯區柏利街三號。畫廊專注於中國早期陶瓷，尤以宋代器物為重，三十餘年來，我們有幸與世界各地的藏家、學者及同行相知相遇，共同探尋那些罕見、神秘而美麗的藝術珍品。

二〇二三年七月，費拉羅女士（Benedicta）的辭世標誌著畫廊的真正終結。沒有本尼，便沒有普利斯特利與費拉羅。我們由衷感謝所有信任我們眼光的藏家——其中許多人，已成為摯友。

大衛·普利斯特利，二〇二六年三月
